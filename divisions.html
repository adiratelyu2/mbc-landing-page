Our Divisions

Specialized teams for specialized challenges.

Cybersecurity

Our cybersecurity division is divided into three teams: the Red Team for cyberattack simulation, the Blue Team for system defense, and the Cloud Team for cloud security.

What we do: Simulating cyberattacks, improving system defenses, and securing cloud-based data and applications.

Big Data

We actively work with Big Data, specializing in Machine Learning to analyze large datasets and extract valuable insights.

What we do: Broad data analysis, insightful predictions, and leveraging Machine Learning techniques.

Game Technology

We focus on multimedia applications, especially Game Technology, utilizing cutting-edge technologies for interactive simulations.

What we do: Creating interactive simulations, developing games, and exploring cutting-edge multimedia applications.

GIS (Geographic Information Systems)

We also focus on Geographic Information Systems (GIS), using spatial data analysis to provide powerful insights.

What we do: Analyzing geographic data, providing spatial insights, and developing GIS applications.
