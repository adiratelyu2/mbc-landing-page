About MBC Laboratory

Multimedia Application, Big Data and Cybersecurity Laboratory

Our Vision & Mission

MBC Laboratory aims to be a leading digital tech research and consulting hub. We're dedicated to boosting our digital visibility, fostering client connections, and showcasing our tech and talent through a secure, modern platform that prioritizes transparency and clear information.

Core Services Overview

We primarily focus on Game Technology and Geographic Information Systems (GIS), using cutting-edge tech for interactive simulations and spatial data analysis. Our Big Data efforts delve into Machine Learning. Our Cybersecurity division has three specialized teams: Red Team (cyberattack simulation), Blue Team (system defense), and Cloud Team (cloud security). We are at the forefront of R&D in these fields.
